About Connect

Connect is Kenya’s platform for quick, reliable services. We empower clients to hire trusted workers for errands, cleaning, deliveries, and more, while helping workers earn fair wages with flexible jobs.

Our Mission

To make everyday tasks easier and create earning opportunities for Kenyans, from Nairobi’s bustling streets to Mombasa’s coastal communities.

Why Choose Connect?

  • Trusted workers verified for safety.
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden fees.
  • Jobs in cities across Kenya, including Kisumu, Eldoret, and Nakuru.
  • Support for both clients and workers to ensure smooth experiences.
