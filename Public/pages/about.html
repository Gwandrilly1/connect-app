About Connect

Connect is Kenya’s go-to platform for reliable, on-demand services. We empower users to hire trusted professionals for errands, cleaning, deliveries, and more.

Our Mission

To simplify everyday life by creating job opportunities and connecting Kenyans to reliable help—anywhere, anytime.

Our Vision

A future where every Kenyan has access to safe, efficient, and dignified work and services at their fingertips.

Core Values

  • Trust: Verified workers, secure transactions.
  • Community: Uplifting local talent.
  • Transparency: No hidden costs or shady practices.
  • Innovation: Smart tech for everyday challenges.
